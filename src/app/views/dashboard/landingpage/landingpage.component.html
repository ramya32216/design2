Why join us?

Put your menu online

Add your menu items and add photos of each dish so that your customers can browse and order without needing to wait to be served.

Reach more customers

Your entire menu becomes searchable citywide. Anyone looking for an item you offer will be attracted to your restaurant.

Return visits

Customers receive in-app notifications every time you change your menu. Any time you post a special or add a new dish, your followers will be notified and encouraged to come and eat!

How does it work?

Customers order

A customer scans the QR code on your table, storefront signage or by searching online within the menuzapp app.

You prepare

You hit the accept button and prepare the order.

Countdown timer

Both you and the customer are synched with a countdown timer that you set andthe customer knows when meal is ready!
